// OUR GOALS AND VISION

WHAT WE STAND FOR

DRIVING EXCELLENCE IN UAV TECHNOLOGY THROUGH A CLEAR SET OF GOALS THAT DEFINE OUR PATH FORWARD.

INNOVATION & RESEARCH

FOCUS ON IDENTIFYING REAL-WORLD UAV CHALLENGES AND DEVELOPING INNOVATIVE DRONE SOLUTIONS THROUGH RESEARCH, PATENTS AND PUBLICATIONS.

COMPETITION EXCELLENCE

PREPARE AND EMPOWER STUDENTS TO PARTICIPATE, COMPETE AND ACHIEVE RECOGNITION IN NATIONAL AND INTERNATIONAL DRONE COMPETITIONS.

INDUSTRY CONNECT

BUILD STRONG INDUSTRY LINKAGES BY PROVIDING INTERNSHIPS, PLACEMENTS, FUNDING OPPORTUNITIES AND INDUSTRIAL EXPOSURE.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

ENCOURAGE STUDENTS TO PROTECT THEIR INNOVATIONS THROUGH IPR, PATENTS AND TECHNOLOGICAL CONTRIBUTIONS.

SKILL DEVELOPMENT

ENHANCE TECHNICAL AND PRACTICAL SKILLS BY DESIGNING, CUSTOMIZING AND BUILDING UAVS THROUGH HANDS-ON LEARNING.

COMMUNITY IMPACT

CREATE A VIBRANT ECOSYSTEM THAT INSPIRES STUDENTS TO EXPLORE DRONE TECHNOLOGY, INNOVATE RESPONSIBLY AND BUILD SUCCESSFUL CAREERS IN THE UAV INDUSTRY.
